- Lifespan: 5-7 years vs 10-15 terrestrial

**3. Data Transmission**
- Downlink bandwidth limited
- Optical/RF bottlenecks

**4. Space Debris**
- 1M satellites = collision nightmare
- Kessler Syndrome risk

**5. Cooling in Vacuum**
- No convection
- Large radiator panels = mass
